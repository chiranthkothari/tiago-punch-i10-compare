Kit & comfort

What each one stands out for

All three share a solid safety core (several items are top-trim only). Beyond that, each has a clear personality.

All threeShared core

  • 6 airbags standard & electronic stability control
  • Touchscreen with CarPlay & Android Auto
  • Rear camera & parking sensors
  • ABS/EBD standard; ISOFIX & hill-hold on higher trims
  • Rear AC vents (Tiago & i10)

Tiago.evThe value EV

  • Cheapest EV here — from ₹6.99 L
  • Very low running cost per km
  • Compact 3,825 mm — easy city parking
  • 360° camera & digital cluster
  • Quiet, gearless drive

Punch.evThe all-rounder

  • Longest range (468 km) & fastest charging
  • Most powerful — 127 hp
  • Single-pane sunroof & ventilated seats
  • SUV stance, 195 mm clearance, roof rails
  • Biggest boot (366 L), air purifier, Alexa

Grand i10The simple choice

  • Lowest price of all — from ₹5.60 L
  • No charging — refuel in ~3 min, ~765 km/tank
  • AMT automatic & bi-fuel CNG options
  • 8" touchscreen, cruise control, wireless charger
  • Wide Hyundai service network
