Kit & comfort

What each one gives you

They share a strong safety and tech core. The differences show up in comfort, wheels and the SUV extras.

SharedOn both cars

  • 10.25" touchscreen, wireless CarPlay & Android Auto
  • Digital instrument cluster
  • 6 airbags & electronic stability control
  • 360° camera & rear parking sensors
  • Hill-hold, ISOFIX, connected-car tech

Tiago onlyHatchback perks

  • Rear AC vents
  • 6-way adjustable driver seat
  • Fabric-finish dashboard
  • Lightest, easiest to park in the city
  • Lowest entry price in Tata's EV range

Punch onlySUV extras

  • Single-pane sunroof
  • Ventilated front seats

  • All-wheel disc brakes, hill descent, roof rails
